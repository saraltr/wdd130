Overview

Purpose

Our goal is to spread culture and history by creating cultural online spaces in the form of websites for cities, museums, and libraries to aid in the promotion of their content and increase audience accessibility.

Audience

Our audience include cultural and historical institutions seeking to establish an online presence, as well as people interested in culture and history, and looking for online cultural and educational spaces.

Content

Home page

At Eculture we are passionate about promoting history and culture while making it available to a wide audience. We want to increase people's access to arts, culture and history, as well as support and diversify their cultural and artistic interests and activities. We consider that cultural heritage is an important part of who we are and that everyone should have access to it, especially in this modern digital time. Online cultural spaces have a significant impact on how we experience culture, not just in terms of access to a larger audience but also in terms of participation, creation, and learning in a digital age. We develop web and mobile applications for cities, museums, and libraries to help them promote their content and make it more accessible to everyone. On our website, you will find the most recent information about ongoing exhibitions, events, and projects in your city, favorite museum, or other cultural spaces. Learn about the ways that technology is transforming our access to culture and the new ways we can learn from it. Learn, share, exchange freely, enrich ourselves with our differences, so many opportunities are possible though this universal communication tool that is the Internet. Our ambition is to build an online space dedicated to celebrate and share our history and culture. We have content for people of all ages!

Blog

Through our official blog, we discuss our latest projects, as well as our favorite artistic and cultural discoveries, including travel, history, museums, music, books, archives and gastronomy. To provide our readers with authentic and engaging cultural experiences, our articles dive deeply into these themes. Our articles highlight seasonal events, activities and festivities that are offered in your community or city so you won't miss out on anything! We also value using interviews to promote initiatives and individuals. We speak with academics as well as individuals from all walks of life who stand out in their own fields and share with us their unique perspectives on the humanities, including philosophers, sociologists, historians, designers, and web content creators. Take part in conversations with others who have similar interests to yours all over the world in the comment sections. Share your opinions on recent history and culture discoveries, and find valuable informations about what you are looking for! Our articles also include the history of Eculture, our own ongoing projects, and any site-related news. We want to have a solid, genuine communication with our clients.
