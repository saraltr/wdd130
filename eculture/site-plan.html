Overview

Purpose

Our goal is to spread culture and history by creating cultural online spaces in the form of websites for cities, museums, and libraries to aid in the promotion of their content and increase audience accessibility.

Audience

Our audience include cultural and historical institutions seeking to establish an online presence, as well as people interested in culture and history, and looking for online cultural and educational spaces.

Content

Home page

At Eculture we are passionate about promoting history and culture while making it available to a wide audience. We want to increase people's access to arts, culture and history, as well as support and diversify their cultural and artistic interests and activities. We consider that cultural heritage is an important part of who we are and that everyone should have access to it, especially in this modern digital time. On our website, you will find the most recent information about ongoing exhibitions, events, and projects in your city, favorite museum, or other cultural spaces. Delve into our history and culture heritage with Eculture! We create cultural online spaces to transform the way visitors experience culture. With our partnership with cities, librairies and professional historians and explore the audiovisual memory of cities and countries all around the world. Our ambition is to build an online space dedicated to celebrate and share our history and culture. We have content for people of all ages!

Learn about the ways that technology is transforming our access to culture and the new ways we can learn from it. Online cultural spaces have a significant impact on how we experience culture, not just in terms of access to a larger audience but also in terms of participation, creation, and learning in a digital age. Learn, share, exchange freely, enrich ourselves with our differences, so many opportunities are possible though this universal communication tool that is the Internet.

Learn the history and cultural heritage of cities, prestigious historic monuments and spaces. Discover how cities develop their own unique cultures and explore key concepts related to cultural heritage. Explore perspectives reconstructed from past eras to learn more about the evolution of human artistic creativity though history.

Increase your understanding of cultural heritage. Cultural heritage extends beyond monuments and object collections. It also includes customs or living expressions that have been passed down from our ancestors, such as oral traditions, performing arts, and many more. Learn more about cultural heritage and share your cultural heritage with people from all over the world.

Blog

Read about our latest projects, as well as our favorite artistic and cultural discoveries, including travel, history, museums, music, books, archives and gastronomy!

Featured Article: New Technologies & Art: The Popularity of Immersive Art Through a thematic, intimate and introspective journey, immersive exhibitions offer unique experience to their visitors. Using animation, videos, text, and visual effects as elements of projections galleries explore approaches to engaging museum visitors. Based on observation as well as interviews of museum visitors, we have analysed how the installation supports sense-making, engaging conversations, and playful engagement. The role of an art center is to decompartmentalize, and this is why digital technology must take its place in 21st century exhibitions. Put at the service of creation, it becomes a formidable vector of dissemination, capable of creating bridges between eras, to make artistic practices vibrate between them, to amplify emotions, to reach as many people as possible. Emphasizing interactivity, immersive exhibitions offer a new way to access art. Discover how historians and specialists also desire to communicate pedagogy though these technology driven exhibitions
