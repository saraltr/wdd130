Welcome

At Eculture we are passionate about promoting history and culture while making it available to a wide audience. We want to increase people's access to arts, culture and history, as well as support and diversify their cultural and artistic interests and activities. We consider that cultural heritage is an important part of who we are and that everyone should have access to it, especially in this modern digital time.

On our website, you will find the most recent information about ongoing exhibitions, events, and projects in your city, favorite museum, or other cultural spaces.

Delve into our history and culture heritage with Eculture! We create cultural online spaces to transform the way visitors experience culture. With our partnership with cities, librairies and professional historians and explore the audiovisual memory of cities and countries all around the world.

Our ambition is to build an online space dedicated to celebrate and share our history and culture. We have content for people of all ages!

Culture & New Technologies

Learn about the ways that technology is transforming our access to culture and the new ways we can learn from it. Online cultural spaces have a significant impact on how we experience culture, not just in terms of access to a larger audience but also in terms of participation, creation, and learning in a digital age. Learn, share, exchange freely, enrich ourselves with our differences, so many opportunities are possible though this universal communication tool that is the Internet.

Learn more about Cultural Heritage

Increase your understanding of cultural heritage. Cultural heritage extends beyond monuments and object collections. It also includes customs or living expressions that have been passed down from our ancestors, such as oral traditions, performing arts, and many more.

Learn more about cultural heritage and share your cultural heritage with people from all over the world.
